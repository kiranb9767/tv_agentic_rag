Streaming contents via AirPlay

You can stream the media files from Apple devices to your TV via AirPlay. Go to  (Home) > Settings > General settings > Apple AirPlay and HomeKit to enter AirPlay and HomeKit Settings page and make sure AirPlay is turned On. Your Apple device must be connected to the same Wi-Fi home network as your TV to stream contents.

 

Add your TV to Apple HomeKit

To add your TV to HomeKit and control your TV via Apple devices:

  1. Go to  (Home) > Settings > General settings > Apple AirPlay and HomeKit to enter AirPlay and HomeKit Settings page.
  2. Select Set UP under HomeKit section to generate a QR code on your TV.
  3. On your Apple device, open the Home app and tap Add  and then scan the QR code on TV.
  4. Follow the instructions on your Apple device and join your TV to HomeKit.
