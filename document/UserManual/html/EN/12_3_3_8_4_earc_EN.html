eARC setting

 (Home) > Settings > Sound > Advanced > eARC

Set eARC mode to Automatic or Off. HDMI eARC (Enhanced Audio Return Channel) is an advancement over the previous ARC. It supports the latest high-bitrate audio formats up to192kHz, 24-bit, and uncompressed 5.1 and 7.1, and 32-channel uncompressed audio.

HDMI eARC is only available on HDMI 1.
