1. Press  SOURCES, select USB and press OK.
  2. Select USB Flash Drive and then select the USB device you need.
  3. Select one of photos, and press OK to view the selected photo.
  • To view photos, select Photos in the menu bar, select a photo thumbnail and press OK.
  • View the photo files with the control menu:
    Playback control bar,  (Jump to the previous photo in a folder),  (Jump to the next photo in a folder),  (Start slideshow playback),  (Pause the playback).
  • Press the icons on player to:
    - Start a slideshow
    - Rotate the photo
    - Shuffle: play your files in a random order
    - Repeat: plays all photos in this folder once or continuously
    - Set the speed of the slide show
    - Stop the music playing in the background.
