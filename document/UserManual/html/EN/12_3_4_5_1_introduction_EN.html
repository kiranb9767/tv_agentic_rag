Ambilight Suite is an integrated control centre to utilize whole Ambilight feature in your daily life. Press  (TV Menu) key on the remote control to find the TV Menu, select Ambilight Suite and press OK to enter the control centre. Alternatively, you can go to  (Home) > Settings > Ambilight > Ambilight Suite and press OK to launch Ambilight Suite.

The Ambilight Suite can switch among Lounge, Sleep and Sunrise styles for different scenarios; each style has its own quick setting items to adjust the settings for you need.
