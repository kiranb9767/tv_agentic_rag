Text language

Some digital TV broadcasters have several Text languages available.

Set teletext preference language

 (Home) > Settings > Region and language > Language > Primary text, Secondary text

Text 2.5

If available, Text 2.5 offers more colours and better graphics. Text 2.5 is activated as a standard factory setting.
To switch off Text 2.5…

  1. Press TEXT or press OPTIONS and then select Teletext.
  2. With Text/Teletext open on screen, press OPTIONS.
  3. Select Text 2.5 > Off to disable Text 2.5.
