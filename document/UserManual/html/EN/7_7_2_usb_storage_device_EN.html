You can view photos or play your music and videos from a connected USB flash drive. Insert a USB flash drive in one of the USB connections on the TV while the TV is switched on.

The TV detects the flash drive and opens a list showing its content.
If the contents list does not appear automatically, press  SOURCES and select USB.

For more information on watching or playing content from a USB flash drive, check on Help > User manual > Photos, Videos and Music.
