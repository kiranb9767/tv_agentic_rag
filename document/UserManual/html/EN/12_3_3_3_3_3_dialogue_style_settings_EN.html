When IntelliSound is set to Custom and Sound style setting is Dialogue, you can go to  (Home) > Settings > Sound > Custom settings to adjust detailed settings for the Dialogue sound style.

 

Vocal boost

 (Home) > Settings > Sound > Custom settings > Vocal boost

Adjust the amount of the dialogue content.

 

Equaliser

 (Home) > Settings > Sound > Custom settings > Equaliser

Adjust the equaliser settings.

 

Reset all to default

 (Home) > Settings > Sound > Custom settings > Reset all to default.

Reset all of Personal settings back to the original factory settings.
