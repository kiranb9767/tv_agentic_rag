Channel list copy and Satellite list copy are intended for dealers and expert users.

With Channel list copy/Satellite list copy, you can copy the channels installed on one TV onto another Philips TV of the same range. You can avoid the time-consuming channel search by uploading a predefined channel list on a TV.

Conditions

  • Both TVs are from the same year range.
  • Both TVs have the same hardware type. Check the hardware type on the TV type plate on the back of the TV.
  • Both TVs have compatible software versions.
  • Use a USB flash drive of minimum 1 GB.
