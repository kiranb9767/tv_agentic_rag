Samba TV makes TV better by cleverly recognising on-screen content. By enabling, you accept and acknowledge Samba TV's Terms of Services and Privacy Policy.

Samba Interactive TV keeps you up to date with the latest TV trends and helps you discover new movies and shows. By analysing what you watch on television, we provide recommendations and other insights through apps such as Samba TV Mobile. Samba TV also enables, either directly or through partners, the delivery of more relevant or personalised content, including ads. You can enable or disable Samba TV in the Privacy Settings option in the TV system settings.
