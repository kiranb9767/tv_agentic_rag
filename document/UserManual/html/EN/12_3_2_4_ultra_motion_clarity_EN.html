 (Home) > Settings > Screen > Ultra motion clarity

Switch on Ultra motion clarity to high refreshing display feature that will allow TV displaying high frame rate video content. It will support 120Hz* VRR gaming. If watching normal video or movie, it will allow TV to apply motion style to have smooth and clear motion effect.

Note:

  • The feature may not be available in some applications or video source.
  • When input video frame rate is 120Hz* (including 120Hz VRR), the feature will be automatically enabled to allow high frame rate display.
    * 120Hz video is supported when HDMI Ultra HD is set to Optimal (Auto Game) or Optimal (Auto Game 120Hz Pro).
  • When this feature is enabled, TV internal processing will limit the effective display resolution to maximum 3840 x 1080p.
