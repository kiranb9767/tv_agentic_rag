To use Alexa, you need to first setup the Alexa client service on your Philips Smart TV. Start the setup by launching the "Amazon Alexa" app from the Home screen of your TV. Follow the instructions and complete the following steps:
- Select an Amazon account and sign-in.
- On your mobile device, link account and discover your devices.
- Return to your TV to complete setup.

Setup on TV for Alexa

  1. Press  Home and open "Amazon Alexa" app.
  2. You need Alexa app (on mobile device or web version) be ready to start the setup.
  3. Follow the instructions on screen to sign in your Amazon account and accept Privacy Policy terms.
  4. On your mobile device, sign in with the same Amazon account in the Amazon Alexa app.
  5. At the end of setup screen, you can select Finished to start to use Alexa.
