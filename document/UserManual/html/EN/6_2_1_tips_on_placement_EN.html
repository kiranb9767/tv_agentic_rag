• Position the TV where light does not shine directly on the screen.
  • Dim lighting conditions in the room for best Ambilight effect.
  • The ideal distance to watch TV is 2 to 5 times its screen height. When seated, your eyes should be level with the centre of the screen.
  • Recommended distance to the wall:
    A. When wall mounted, position the TV 7 to 10 cm away from the wall.
    B. When TV is with stand, position the TV 10 to 20 cm away from the wall depends on the size of stand.

Note:

Please keep the TV away from dust-producing sources such as stoves. It is suggested to clean the dust regularly to avoid getting dust into the TV.
